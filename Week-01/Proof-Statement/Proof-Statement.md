# What Are You Proving?

## Proof Statement

My portfolio proves that I can build practical AI applications that solve real problems using modern AI tools, Python, and machine learning workflows. It is designed for an AI engineering hiring manager at an AI startup who wants evidence that I can turn ideas into working projects rather than just complete coursework. After reviewing my work, I want them to invite me for an AI/ML internship interview because the projects demonstrate my ability to design, build, and ship AI solutions from concept to deployment.

## Why this portfolio needs to exist

My resume and LinkedIn list my skills and experiences, but they cannot prove that I can independently build and deliver complete AI projects. This portfolio provides that proof through real, working projects.
